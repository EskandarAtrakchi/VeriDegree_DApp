HOW TO USE
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DOWNLOAD & SETUP:
  1. Click "Download ZIP" (top right of v0)
  2. Extract ZIP to your computer
  3. Open terminal in extracted folder
  4. Run: pnpm install
  5. Run: cp .env.local.example .env.local
  6. Run: pnpm dev
  7. Open: http://localhost:3000

TESTING FLOW:
  1. Connect MetaMask wallet
  2. Get Sepolia testnet ETH (free faucet)
  3. Issue a certificate
  4. Verify the certificate with student address
  5. View your certificates

DEBUGGING:
  1. Open DevTools: F12
  2. Go to Console tab
  3. Look for [IPFS], [Web3] logs
  4. Check TROUBLESHOOTING_PINATA.md for issues
  5. All error messages logged clearly
